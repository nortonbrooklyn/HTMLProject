BROOKLYN'S TRAVEL ADVENTURES

AUSTRALIA

Trip Highlights

  1. Snorkeling at the Great Barrier Reef
    • Saw lots of cool fish
    • Fun boat ride out to the reefs
  2. Driving down the Great Ocean Road
    • Saw lots of koalas along the road
    • Visited the 12 apostles
  3. Hanging out with fun animals
    • Got to pet lots of kangaroos
    • Cokatoos landed on our hands



ARGENTINA

Trip Highlights

  1. Going to the beach in La Costa
    • Refreshing, cold water
    • Good sand to build sand castles
  2. Visiting the temple in Buenos Aires
    • Took pretty pictures outside
    • Walked through the gardens
  3. Touring the Cathedral in La Plata
    • Lots of pretty paintings inside
    • Incredible architecture



MEXICO

Trip Highlights

  1. Seeing turtles while snorkeling
    • Turtles were interactive
    • There were lots of them
  2. Visiting the ancient ruins at Tulum
    • Learned a lot about Mayan history
    • Could climb on the different structures
  3. Relaxing on the beach
    • Read some books while soaking in the sun
    • Got super tan



HAWAII

Trip Highlights

  1. Helicopter ride over Kauai
    • Beautiful views of the mountains
    • Saw the whole island in under an hour
  2. Polynesian Cultural Center
    • Learned a lot about the different Polynesian islands
    • Got to participate in a true Hawaiin Luau
  3. Mountain tubing through caves
    • Relaxing yet also thrilling at some parts
    • Fun tour guide that told lots of jokes
